After years of not being on any social media, I realized there was no good
way for people to know what's going on in my life. So I made this site.

Here you'll find **life updates** (like this one), the **personal projects**
I'm working on, and **photos** from trips and everyday moments. No ads, no
infinite scroll, no algorithm deciding what you see.

If you want to follow along, you can bookmark [the updates page](/updates)
or subscribe to the [RSS feed](/updates/rss.xml) in a reader. Otherwise,
just check back whenever you're curious. 👋
